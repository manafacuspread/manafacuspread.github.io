The website where nobody can use "neologism" and invalidate real terms as just internet-made custom terms and just nonsense.

verb • /mah-nah-fah-kyoo-spred/
Definition (not a slang term)
The act of accidentally or intentionally distributing an absolute, genuine truth in a format, style, or tone that looks so artificial, sketchy, or AI-generated that audiences automatically reject it as a hoax.
Etymology
  • Mana – From the Latin mendacium (lie)
  • Facu – From the Latin factum (truth)
  • Spread – To distribute
Example

"The whistleblower had real documents, but because they dumped them on a sketchy, unformatted forum, they ended up manafacuspreading the whole story."
